Notes on ‘Statistical Inference’ by Casella & Berger

2022-03-07

Preface

These are my notes on the second edition of George Casella and Roger Berger’s ‘Statistical Inference.’ They are incomplete, I’m still working my way through the book. I’m broadly following this handbook by Oliver Y. Chén which goes through each chapter and highlights the important concepts and theorems, as well as suggesting some questions to attempt. However most of my chapter notes so far include a little more detail than is suggested by the handbook, as well as some plots and animations to present some of the points visually.
